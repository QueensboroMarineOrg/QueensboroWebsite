Our Process

Over our company history, we have been entrusted us with hundreds of projects. We've serious about the following:

• A design the client loves: Our design process is focused around your satisfaction. During the first stages we prototype 3D mockups to ensure a flawless design prior to production. We’ll involve you in this process to make sure you’re needs are met.
• A product that lasts, and is enjoyed by the customer: During the fabrication process, we manufacture with the aid of industry professionals to provide an up-to-date and cost effective solution. We also build our docks to be environmentally friendly, allowing light to pass through and not disturb marine life.
• Installation that's on-budget & on-time Finally, during the installation process we are serious about delivering on the proposal in a timely fashion, and meeting the project's goals.

50+ years of experience across the industry

Queensboro was originally located on the Fraser River, and has since relocated to Scotch Creek, in the BC Interior. Our company is ISO 9001:2008 registered, and has grown to offer dozens of services for oilfields, manufacturing, and marine applications.

Services we offer beyond docks/marina installation/fabrication are:
• Water treatment 
• Oil spill response
• Fishing equipment
• Storage containres & tanks
• Water management system
